FertiLedger — Fertilizer Shop Ledger App
==========================================

HOW TO INSTALL ON ANDROID (as App):
-------------------------------------
1. Copy the FertiLedger folder to your phone
2. Open Chrome browser on your Android phone
3. Open the index.html file (or serve it locally)
4. Tap the 3-dot menu → "Add to Home Screen"
5. App icon appears on your home screen!

HOW TO USE ON PC/LAPTOP:
--------------------------
Simply double-click index.html to open in browser.
Works completely offline — no internet needed after first load.

FEATURES:
----------
- Multiple vendor management
- Purchase entries (Debit) with receipt number, date, product description, amount
- Payment entries (Credit) to track what you have paid
- Vendor-wise outstanding balance tracking
- Monthly summary reports
- Vendor-wise reports
- Product-wise reports
- Date range search and filtering
- Print/receipt generation for each entry
- Export/Import data as JSON backup
- Works 100% offline

ALL DATA IS STORED LOCALLY on your device (never sent anywhere).

For online hosting (recommended for install-as-app):
- Upload index.html, sw.js, manifest.json to any free host like:
  - GitHub Pages (free)
  - Netlify (free)
  - Vercel (free)
